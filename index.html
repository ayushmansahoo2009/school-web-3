  • About Dav public School

    Twenty-eight years on, DAV Public School, Rajabagicha has been a safe choice for students in Cuttack. Established in 1990 and managed by the DAV College Trust and Management Society, New Delhi, the school is housed in a large building in the centre of the city in a calm and pleasant environment. It is home to the tiny tots, primary and middle school students. It is a co-educational institution which prepares children to face the world with the courage of conviction and forthright attitude to realize Swami Dayananda's deal. It believes in integrity, honesty and sincerity of work. This institution has crossed many a milestone but it has remained firmly committed to nurturing young minds to meet the challenges of this millennium. DAV Rajabagichas centre of the world is the child and every step he takes is the schools' satisfaction. Hence a sound academic education is reinforced with plenty of co-curricular activities. The Indian and western pattern has helped foster a generation of progressive young men and women ready to contribute to the growth and development and progress of our country. The school not only produces academically brilliant students but createS human beings with sacred Indian values. Children stay rooted to Indian culture while at the same time keeping their mind open to global thoughts and innovations. Performing 'Havans' ensure that children keep in touch with Vedic practices. Through performing Vedic practices the institution tries to fulfill Swami Dayananda's vision of being rooted to Indian culture and tradition. The best of Education and facilities are provided at an affordable price to help the students to live up to their dreams irrespective of their caste, creed and religion. All efforts are made towards the holistic improvement of each individual student. From a sprawling campus providing both education and extracurricular activities, fun fests and exhibitions DAV Rajabagicha is a dream place for every student aspiring for holistic growth.
